Meet Meenu

Real, practical, and deeply personal

Hi, I'm Meenu Shevetha. I'm so glad you're making time to read this! Honestly, I didn't set out to be a nutritionist. I just wanted to feel good in my own body, have energy that lasts, and enjoy food without guilt. That curiosity turned into a career with 6 years of learning both clinical and fitness nutrition. My approach has always stayed the same: real, practical, and deeply personal.

I've worked with clients taking their first steps towards health, busy professionals juggling work and wellness, and celebrities and athletes chasing peak performance. What I love most is seeing the shift, not just in numbers on a scale, but in confidence, energy, and how life feels every day.

At Sustenhance, it's not about quick fixes. It's about helping you find balance, feel empowered, and create habits that actually stick, because lasting health isn't a diet, it's a way of living you can love.
