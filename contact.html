Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

What happens in our first session?

Our first session is typically 45-60 minutes. We'll review your health history, current eating habits, lifestyle, goals, and any medical conditions or lab results you bring. By the end, you'll leave with an initial action plan, all your queries answered, and not just a list of things to avoid.

How is a dietician different from a nutritionist?

"Dietician" is a protected, regulated title requiring specific education, supervised practice, and licensing/certification. "Nutritionist" isn't always regulated the same way, anyone can technically use that title in some regions, regardless of formal training.

How many sessions will I need?

It totally depends on your goals. Some people see meaningful progress in 3-4 months, others, especially those managing a chronic condition or working toward long-term habit change, benefit from ongoing monthly check-ins over 6-12 months.

How often will we connect?

Most plans start with weekly or biweekly calls, then space out to monthly as you build consistency and confidence. But everyday monitoring will be happening via our trackers and WhatsApp.

What if I have food allergies, intolerances, or cultural/religious dietary needs?

All plans are built around your needs: allergies, intolerances, budget, cooking skill, and cultural or religious practices are factored in from the start, not treated as an afterthought.

Can you help with a specific medical condition (diabetes, PCOS, IBS, high cholesterol, etc.)?

Yes, I specialize in diabetes, hormonal issues like PCOS, thyroid, cholesterol issues, deficiency management, and gastric issues. Nutrition therapy can support management of many conditions, often working alongside your doctor's treatment plan.

Do you help with weight loss?

Yes, weight-related goals are part of the plan, and the focus will also be on sustainable habits, health markers, and quality of life, not quick fixes or extreme restriction.

Do you treat eating disorders?

Yes, I have worked with a number of clients on eating disorders and helped them build the healthy eating patterns they deserve to have.

Do you offer packages or payment plans?

Every plan, from the package to the diet, is completely customized. There are no generic or standard market packages. Once your consultation is done, the packages will be completely customized, aligned to your goals and the services required to give you the best results.

How soon will I see results?

This varies by person and goal. Some notice changes in energy or digestion within a couple of weeks; changes in lab markers or weight typically take longer and depend on consistency. But you would reach your goals, and that would be my promise!

What if I don't like what you recommend?

Plans are collaborative, not prescriptive. If something doesn't fit your lifestyle, taste, or budget, it gets adjusted. The plan should work for you, not the other way around.

Is this a "diet," will I have to give up foods I love?

The approach isn't about banning foods. It's about building a sustainable relationship with eating that includes the foods you enjoy, in a way that supports your goals.
