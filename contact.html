Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How does a consultation actually work?

You book a free discovery call, Meenu learns about your goals, history, and lifestyle, then puts together a plan tailored specifically to you, not a generic template. Ongoing check-ins keep the plan adjusted as you progress.

Is every plan really personalized, or is it a template?

Every plan is built from scratch around your assessment and consultation, your medical history, food preferences, culture, schedule, and goals all shape it. Nothing is copy-pasted from a generic diet chart.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes, all consultations are online, so you can work with Meenu from anywhere in the world.

What conditions do you specialize in?

Clinical nutrition for diabetes, PCOS, gut health, renal, heart health, and more, alongside sports nutrition, weight management, prenatal and family nutrition, and fully bespoke plans.

I'm not sure what I need, can I still book a call?

Absolutely. Try the free assessment first for a starting recommendation, or just book a discovery call and Meenu will help you figure out the right starting point.
