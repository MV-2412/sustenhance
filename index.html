1,000+ individuals guided across hospitals, sports academies, and private practice

Over the years, I've worked across government hospitals, private healthcare, sports academies, gyms, and nutrition clinics, helping over 1,000 individuals from every walk of life reclaim their health.

Most diets ignore hormones, medical history, culture, lifestyle, and emotion, then blame the person for not fitting the plan. I don't hand you a meal plan and ask for discipline, I listen to what your body is trying to communicate, and build something that works with you.

My clients come from very different lives: professionals managing PCOS on impossible schedules, new mothers rebuilding strength, athletes chasing peak performance. Almost every consultation opens the same way: "I'm tired of generic advice that doesn't fit my life."

I don't believe healing means giving up the food you grew up loving, or that health should feel like punishment. Sustainable results come from understanding why your body feels the way it does, guided by science, empathy, and honesty.

No two bodies are the same. So no two nutrition journeys should be either.
